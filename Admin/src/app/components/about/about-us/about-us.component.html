Our Mission
- Providing medicine for patients quickly and inexpensively, delivering the drug in the least time and at the lowest cost, helping the customer to know the nearest pharmacy where the medicine he wants is available and with the medication pamphlet.
- The possibility of ordering medicine online.
- Providing a platform for each pharmacy to determine the quantities and available drugs based on available databases.
Company philosophy and vision
- The first site to request medicine in Egypt and the Middle East by connecting the largest possible number of pharmacies and hospitals to provide the necessary medicine and continuous follow-up.
Company goals & Target market
- The goal is to develop the site to deliver services in all parts of Egypt at the local level approximately 100 million people.
- The main target in the beginning is the residents of Alexandria Governorate.
Industry
- The site aims to spread first in the governorate of Alexandria in all the surrounding regions and then expand to include Egypt completely by providing the fastest way to obtain medicine confidently and effectively and to ensure the satisfaction of patients and to provide the necessary from local and imported medicine from pharmaceutical companies and pharmacies.
